What is Backed?

A game where real business news decides who wins.

You build a team of real entrepreneurs and CEOs. Every week, your team is matched against another player's team. The score is based entirely on what happens in the real world that week: if Tesla's stock drops 5%, Elon Musk loses power. If Spotify announces record growth, Daniel Ek surges. At the end of the week, the team with more power wins, and you earn tokens you can use to make your team even stronger.

How to play

Four steps.

01

Scout the roster

Browse real business leaders. Each one has a live power score based on their stock price, what's being written about them in the news, and how other players are betting on them.

02

Draft your team

Pick 5 people you believe in. Your team score is the combined power of your fighters, updated in real time.

03

Watch them fight

Every Monday, you're matched against an opponent. During the week, real events shift the score: earnings reports, product launches, viral headlines, scandals. You see exactly what's happening and why.

04

Win and grow

Win a match and earn 5 bonus tokens. Beat someone ranked above you and earn 7. The more you win, the stronger your team gets and the higher you climb, from Bronze all the way to Legend.

How scores work

Every score has a reason.

There's no luck and no hidden algorithm. Each player's weekly power comes from three things you can verify yourself. After every match, you see exactly which events moved the score and why.

40%
Stock price

How their company's stock moved this week compared to everyone else on the roster. If NVIDIA went up 8% and that's in the top 10% of all players, Jensen Huang scores high.

40%
News momentum

Not just how much is written, but whether it changed. If someone suddenly gets a wave of negative press they don't normally get, their score drops.

20%
Community bets

How other players are betting. Here's the twist: the more popular a pick, the less they're worth. Everyone drafts Jensen Huang? His impact drops. Find someone nobody else sees, and you score big.

Competition

Play with friends or against the world.

Matches run every week, Monday to Sunday. You can follow the score all week as real events happen. On Sunday night, the match is settled and you see the full breakdown.

Private leagues

Create a league and invite your friends with a code. Everyone builds a team, everyone gets matched up each week, and there's a league table showing who's on top. This is where the trash talk lives.

Global ranking

Every player has an ELO rating that goes up when you win and down when you lose, just like in chess. Beat someone ranked higher than you and you climb fast. There are five ranks: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, and Legend.
